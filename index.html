Who We Are

A Legacy of Iron & Steel

For over a decade, Abdul Manan Merzai & Brothers has reshaped the skyline and infrastructure of Pakistan. We approach heavy construction with absolute precision, zero compromise on safety, and a grit that matches the materials we work with.

From deep foundations to soaring high-rises, national highway networks to robust industrial complexes, we operate as a full-service contracting and engineering powerhouse. Our vertical integration ensures that every machinery unit, engineer, and raw asset aligns with strict international standards, enabling us to deliver complex projects on budget and ahead of schedule.
